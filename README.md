Panacea

Panacea is a web application designed to help individuals manage their medical information in one secure, organized place. It focuses on reducing confusion, missed medications, and scattered health records by giving users a single, simple dashboard for their healthcare data.

The Problem

Many people struggle with:

Forgetting to take medications on time

Managing multiple prescriptions

Storing medical documents across phones, chats, and paper files

Quickly accessing health records during emergencies or doctor visits

These gaps often lead to poor adherence, stress, and avoidable health risks.

The Solution

Panacea centralizes essential health information so users can focus on their well-being instead of tracking details manually.

Key Features

Medication management with scheduled reminders

Add, update, or remove active medications easily

Secure upload and organization of medical documents such as prescriptions, scans, and reports

Clean, minimal interface for quick access to critical information

Web-first design with future support for mobile app deployment

Who It’s For

Individuals managing long-term or multiple medications

Caregivers tracking health data for family members

Anyone who wants a reliable digital health record system

Panacea aims to be a simple, practical tool that brings clarity and consistency to personal healthcare management.
